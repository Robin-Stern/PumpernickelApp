### CURRENT Row Container Color

The CURRENT rank row uses `MaterialTheme.colorScheme.primaryContainer.copy(alpha = 0.55f)` as its container color. PASSED and LOCKED rows use `MaterialTheme.colorScheme.surfaceVariant.copy(alpha = 0.5f)` (matching the OverviewRankStrip Card container). This gives a subtle but clear visual distinction for the user's active rank.
